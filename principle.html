Principle of Operation

Based on repeated reflection of light. Mirrors (or prisms) at 45° angles reflect light twice, shifting the view vertically while keeping it upright and parallel.

Light enters top → reflects down → reflects horizontally to eye. Field of view depends on tube length & width.

Submarine Variant

Uses prisms + lenses for magnification and image erection (correct orientation). Many include rangefinders and cameras today.
