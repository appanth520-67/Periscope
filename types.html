Main Categories

  • Simple mirror periscope – Cardboard tube + two mirrors (DIY, toys, parades)
  • Prism periscope – Uses reflecting prisms instead of mirrors (better light transmission)
  • Submarine periscope – Complex with lenses, zoom, rotation, rangefinding
  • Tank / armored vehicle periscope – 360° rotatable driver's optics
  • Modern electro-optical – Digital cameras, thermal imaging, no direct eyepiece
